worldcup-predict

World Cup prediction with animals

Examples:

$ worldcup-predict TEAM1 TEAM2

  _______________
 | TEAM1 > TEAM2 |
  ---------------
   \
    \
        .--.
       |o_o |
       |:_/ |
      //   \ \
     (|     | )
    /'\_   _/`\
    \___)=(___/
